Things to Do in San Francisco

Chinatown

Golden Gate Bakery

One of the most popular bakeries in Chinatown, and is most famous for its delicious egg tarts!

Hours:

  • Mon-Sun: 8 AM-8 PM

Dragon's Gate

If you go to Chinatown, you should definitely stop by this iconic entrance for some photos!

Hon's Wun-Tun House

Affordable aand delicious wonton noodles!

Hours:
  • Mon-Sat: 10 AM-7 PM
  • Sun: CLOSED

Fisherman's Wharf

Pier 39

This popular tourist attraction contains shops, restaurants, and sea lions on the docks! If you're lucky! You may also catch street performances!

Boudin Bakery

Known for its sourdough bread, this oldest continually operating business in San Francisco is my favorite place to get a clam chowder bowl.

Hours:

  • Mon-Sat: 12 AM-9 PM

Ride the Cable Car

If you've never been on a San Francisco cable car, I highly recommend doing so! Enjoy San Francisco's sights as the wind blows through your hair!

Hours:

  • Mon-Sun: 7 AM-10:30 PM

Japantown

Daiso

Yes, there are Daiso's in many places, but I always love a good Daiso run and the one in Japantown is very big and filled wth cheap, cute goodies!

Hours:

  • Mon-Sun: 10 AM-7 PM

Izumi Kaiten Sushi

Sushi is delivered on a conveyer belt, and the variety and taste is great!

Hours:

  • Mon, Wed, Thu: 12 PM-9 PM
  • Tue: 11 AM-7 PM
  • Fri: 12 PM-9 PM
  • Sat-Sun: 11:30 AM-9 PM
